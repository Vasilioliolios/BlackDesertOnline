Black desert online has a multitude of classes. This is just a small compendium of available classes and all of their strengths and weaknesses. Each class in bdo has a specific role when it comes to large guild fights (i.e. 100 man guild vs 100 man guild) and it is imperative that each class does their duty when in the fight, otherwise you could lose the battle. contact us.

Player vs. Player Combat

Guilds in Black Desert online can fight for the actual land that everyone plays on. Every saturday there is siege warfare from 9pm eastern to 12am eastern. Multiple guilds can place on the same territory, and each guild can bring up to 100 people. Sieges can get insane with the number of people on the node, some guilds form alliances until they are the last ones standing and then fight to the end and the last guild standing is the winner of the territory. After siege there a siege podcast between all the guilds that fought on all 5 territories and then they discuss and laugh about everything that happened. Sometimes there is some bitterness between them, and a fight erupts. All part of the game.
